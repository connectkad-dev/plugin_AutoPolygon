Automatic creation of a polygon with a point
When you do a left click, a rectangle is created with the bottom left point of the rectangle as the default reference. (This reference may be changed in the options) If you leave the left click pressed, you can rotate the rectangle.
Automatic creation of a polygon with a line
When you do a left click, a line with rectangle is created. This line can be only with two points. To validate the line with rectangle, you press the right click. If you leave the right click pressed, you can choose the rectangle's orientation by moving the cursor right or left of the line.
Options
You can choose the width, the height and the position of reference.
